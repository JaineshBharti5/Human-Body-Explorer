Heart

Circulatory System
300g · Chest

Your heart is a powerful pump the size of your fist that never takes a break. It beats about 100,000 times every single day!

Think of it as a double pump. The right side collects "used" blood and sends it to the lungs. The left side takes fresh blood and pumps it to every part of your body.
